About

A decade in recruitment. Now inside the companies I used to hire for.

I started in recruitment at Hays Recruitment in January 2010, then founded Priority Recruitment in 2012 and built it into one of the UK's leading Web3 talent firms. In 2019 I launched Priority Crypto, placing engineering, marketing and executive talent across the Solana, Polkadot and Ethereum ecosystems.

In 2025 I joined Raiku Labs as their first People & Culture hire, building the entire function from scratch across a 23-person globally distributed team. That perspective, having been the recruiter and now the operator, is what Beyond Culture is built on.
